# Research: Google AI Content & Adult Site Indexing Strategy (2025-2026)

## Context

I'm about to publish AI-generated content pages on a zero-authority domain (brand new .com, no backlinks, no history). The site is a live cam roulette platform, and the content engine will generate:
- 2,500 model profile pages (400-500 words each)
- Eventually 5,000+ blog posts (1,500-2,500 words)
- Category hub pages

All content is AI-generated via Claude API, with human review of quality gates. The pages are pre-rendered static HTML (fast TTFB, no JS rendering required).

**My planned rollout:**
- Week 1: 20 model pages → submit to Search Console → monitor
- Week 2: 50 more if no issues
