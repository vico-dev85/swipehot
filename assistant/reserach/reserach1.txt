### Step 2: You Write Research Prompts

For each research topic, create a **detailed research prompt** that I can copy-paste to ChatGPT or Claude web.

Each prompt should:
- ✅ Be self-contained (works without context)
- ✅ Specify exactly what to search for
- ✅ Ask for specific, actionable findings
- ✅ Request sources/citations
- ✅ Define output format clearly

**Example format for each file in `todo/`:**
```markdown
# Research: TikTok-Style Video UX

## Context
We're building a video roulette app similar to TikTok's infinite scroll. Users swipe through random live cam performers. We need to understand what makes TikTok's UX so addictive and how to apply those principles.

## Research Questions

### 1. Technical Implementation
- How does TikTok's infinite scroll work technically?
- What's their video preloading strategy? (how many ahead?)
- How do they handle smooth transitions between videos?
- What's the technical architecture for instant swipes?

**Search queries to use:**
- "TikTok infinite scroll implementation"
- "TikTok video preloading architecture"
- "TikTok clone technical tutorial"
- "how TikTok works technically"

### 2. Psychological Engagement
- What psychological principles make "just one more swipe" so compelling?
- How does TikTok's algorithm maximize watch time?
- What's the dopamine loop mechanism?
- How do they balance novelty vs personalization?

**Search queries to use:**
- "TikTok psychology addiction"
- "infinite scroll dopamine loop"
- "TikTok engagement psychology"
- "why TikTok is addictive"

### 3. UX Patterns & Best Practices
- What are the specific UX patterns TikTok uses?
- How do they handle loading states?
- What makes the swipe gesture feel good?
- How do they prevent jank/lag?

**Search queries to use:**
- "TikTok UX best practices"
- "video app smooth scrolling"
- "TikTok interaction design"
- "TikTok UI patterns"

### 4. Open Source Examples
- Are there any open-source TikTok clones?
- What frameworks/libraries do they use?
- What can we learn from their code?

**Search queries to use:**
- "TikTok clone open source"
- "TikTok clone GitHub"
- "infinite scroll video app tutorial"
- "TikTok alternative source code"

## Output Format

Please provide your findings in this format:

### Finding 1: [Specific insight]
**Source:** [URL or article title]
**Key points:**
- [Specific actionable point]
- [Specific actionable point]
**Recommendation for our project:**
- [How we should apply this]

### Finding 2: [Specific insight]
[Same format]

... (continue for all findings)

## Summary
[2-3 paragraph summary of the most important insights]

## Actionable Recommendations
1. [Specific thing we should implement]
2. [Specific thing we should avoid]
3. [Pattern/library/approach we should use]

---

**Estimated research time:** 30-45 minutes
**Priority:** High (affects core UX architecture)
```
